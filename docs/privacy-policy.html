Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Kamyabi Cash ("we", "our", "us") operates the Kamyabi Cash mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Phone Number: Used for account creation and authentication via Firebase Phone Auth.
  • Device Information: Hardware identifiers (device model, manufacturer, Android ID, screen resolution, RAM) for security and fraud prevention. We do NOT collect IP addresses for device identification.
  • Usage Data: Task completions, gaming sessions, ad interactions, referral activity, and transaction history.
  • Advertising ID: Used by our ad partners to serve relevant advertisements.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process coin rewards, withdrawals, and transfers
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and multi-accounting
  • To serve advertisements through our ad network partners
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To communicate important updates about your account

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics
  • AdMob (Google): Advertisement serving
  • AppLovin: Advertisement serving and mediation
  • Meta Audience Network: Advertisement serving
  • Adjoe, TapJoy, OfferToro: Gaming offerwall platforms

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Server-side coin validation to prevent tampering
  • Device fingerprinting for fraud prevention
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Security Rules for database access control

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@kamyabicash.com
